How to Use XREX Pay Bot
Link your account

Start by linking your Telegram account with your XREX Web App for secure access to features

Check wallet risk

Use /check_wallet <address> and paste a blockchain wallet address to instantly view its exchange affiliation, on-chain balances, and risk level

OTC trading

Request real-time OTC quotes using /otc_quote and track orders with /otc_orders

Receive notifications

Get instant updates in 1:1 chats or group chats whenever your OTC order status changes
